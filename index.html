What is BernHack ?

BernHack is BernHardt College’s first-ever programming event organized by students, for students all over Nepal. We are MLH’s (Major League Hacking) first ever member hackathon in Nepal. The event is aimed to bring together all young hacking enthusiasts under one roof.

Reputed mentors and trainers will oversee the participants. Our event will house a huge network of professionals, business delegates along with active youths.

BernHack will last up to two days which will include:

  • Address from professionals from different IT field
  • Mentorship from the professionals of the field
  • Speakers giving information on topics of related field
  • Time to network, connect with people and find inspiration to continue with hacking

FAQs
Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
BernHack is offline event and as a participant you need to be present at Kathmandu BernHardt College before the event.
Do this hackathon has any participation fees for students?
No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over Nepal.
What are all the restrictions on Team formations for the event?
A team can have at most 4 members and minimum of 2 members.
What should I do if I do not have any team yet?
Just fill out the application form and according to skills you have filled out, we will match you with the best team possible during Pre-Hackathon meeting.
What are the Perks and Benefits of Participating in this Hackathon?
Hackathon Provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the Projects like Blockchain, AI, Machine Learning, etc. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshments to keep them up.
What are the Accommodation Facility for the Participant from Outside the College?
Participant will be provided with Accommodation and Food at the College during the Hackathon Period.
